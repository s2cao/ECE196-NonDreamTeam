The Dangers of Drowsy Driving

Excessive Daytime Sleepiness (EDS) is a disorder that affects 20% of the global population. While this may seem like at most an inconvenience in daily life, there are certain aspects that those without this disorder take for granted. When EDS afflicted people are driving, it's common for them to dose off during long periods of monotany, such as a long highway. This results in a lack of focus and dangerous driving.

The HypnoGlove: A smart glove that can recognize when you're falling asleep and alert you in various ways to keep you focused on the road.

This glove uses R-R Interval Detection and Sampling to detect abnormalities in Heartrate and recognize drowsiness in the user.



Flowchart of the HypnoGlove state

The Hypnoglove is our solution to driving with EDS. This glove can detect when you are drifting to sleep by measuring fluctuations and abnormailities in the R-R intervals of your heartbeat. When it detects these factors related to drowsiness, it will provide stimuli in the form of blinking lights, sound cues, and vibration to wake the driver*.
